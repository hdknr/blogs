## Wordpress Development Environment

- `vim wp-cli.yml`
- `./scaffold.bash`
- `sudo $(phpenv prefix)/sbin/php-fpm -y conf/php-fpm.conf`
- `sudo ln -s $PWD/conf/nginx.conf /etc/nginx/sites-enabled/your_wp_cname.conf`
- `sudo /etc/init.d/nginx start`
